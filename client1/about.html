About Ping Pong Pizza Place

Our Mission
Ever since he was a kid, our founder, Rodney Foreman has loved pizza, but he especially loved going out to dinner with his family at their local pizza place. Now, Rodney wants every kid to be able to experience an outing at their pizza place of choice. Here at Ping Pong Pizza Place we are all about the family experience inside our establishment. From the lavish Arcade area where kids may enjoy many classic games such as, Tetris, Racing, Super Mario, and many more! For children of smaller ages and those that prefer to be more active we offer an extensive play area with slides, crawlspaces, and assorted wall games throughout. Of course the adults are not simply left to their own devices, we have mounted wall tv's playing anything from sports to cartoons depending on time of day and ambient music to soothe your eating experience. Should the occassion for a visit be a birthday, let our cashier know once you have arrived at the restaurant in order for us to set up a surprise "Happy Birthday" song!
